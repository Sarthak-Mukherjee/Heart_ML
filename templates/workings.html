To estimate your chances of having a heart attack, please fill out the following fields:

  • Age: Enter your current age in years.
  • Gender: Select either Male(enter 1) or Female(enter 0).
  • Impulse: Your heart impulse or pulse rate (beats per minute).
  • Pressure High: Your systolic blood pressure (upper value).
  • Pressure Low: Your diastolic blood pressure (lower value).
  • Glucose: Your current blood glucose level (mg/dL).
  • Troponin: Your troponin protein level ‚Äî a key marker used in heart attack prediction.

After filling out all fields, click the Predict button below. The application will process the data and display whether your chances are Positive or Negative based on your inputs.

Note: This tool is not a diagnostic test. It offers a data-based prediction, but for any medical concerns, please consult a healthcare professional.
